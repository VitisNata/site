    • Bianca

      The grape was officially register for use in wine production in 1982 and today is used to make a wide assortment of wines from dry varietals to sweet dessert wines.

      Bianca is growing in popularity among organic vineyards due to its natural high resistance to many fungal diseases that affect grapevines.
